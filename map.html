Track Map

  • Instructions▾
    • Create a Track

    1. Tap on the map to start drawing a track, with each tap creating a connecting line

    2. When you're done, make sure to tap 'end track'.

    3. Find your new track's unique ID under 'Track Settings'!

    Load/Delete a Track

    1. Enter your track number via your profile or email

    2.a. Tap 'Load'. Your track should then automatically load on the map!

    2.b. Tap 'Delete'. Your track will be removed from the database.

    You will be alerted if the track doesn't exist. You may need to retry the load if it doesn't appear.
